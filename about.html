About Us

Hunger Heroes

Hunger Heroes is a registered nonprofit organization that provides a webbased "marketplace" allowing excess food to be donated from the food service industry to qualified charities that work with the needy. One-third of all food is wasted from farms, restaurant, grocery stores and individual households. Yet, there has been no efficient way for groups that have excess food to donate to the millions of hungry Nigerians. Hunger Heroes is that medium. Donors who wish to donate their excess food can sign up and post excess food as it becomes available. Qualified charities in the vicinity of the donors who have been vetted by Hunger Heroes can claim the food. The charities are responsible for transportation and food handling.

Hunger Heroes is an incredible organization that aims at promoting the United Nations Sustainable Development Goal 2, which is Zero Hunger. ...It was created due to the need to alleviate the societal problem of hunger in our society, to end food wastage, fight food insecurity and tackle malnutrition challenge. There are a lot of hungry people around, people who cannot afford at least one meal a day. Sadly, so many children fall under this category.

WHY SHOULD YOU SUPPORT HUNGER HEROES?
We are the nation's largest food donation organization. The hunger and malnutrition scourge is daily assuming a monstrous dimension in Nigeria. The statistics are as horrendous and alarming as they come. With the donations and support from businesses, government organizations and individuals like you, Hunger Heroes Network of Food bank, pantries and meal programs, we serve virtually every hungry person in the society.
